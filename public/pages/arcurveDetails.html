Project Overview

The Arcurve website redesign was a comprehensive project undertaken during my internship to modernize the company's online presence. The goal was to create a website that not only looked contemporary but also accurately represented Arcurve's culture, values, and technical capabilities.

Working closely with UX and UI designers, I was responsible for the full development lifecycle. From initial research and design planning through implementation and deployment. The project required balancing technical implementation with user-centered design principles to create a site that would effectively engage potential clients. While leveraging Webflow as a low-code website builder for efficiency, I also coded bespoke elements from scratch when custom functionality or unique design requirements called for it.

User Research

Conducted extensive user research and created detailed personas and journey mappings to understand client needs and optimize the user experience.

CMS Migration

Migrated the company to a new CMS platform and consolidated all dynamic content into one easily managed form, streamlining content management.

Client Engagement

Designed the site to maximize contacts with potential clients through improved user flows and strategic content placement.

Project Outcomes

The Arcurve website redesign successfully achieved its goals of modernizing the company's online presence and improving client engagement.

  • Modern Brand Representation: The new website accurately reflects Arcurve's culture and services, creating a more authentic and professional online presence.
  • Improved User Experience: Through extensive user research and journey mapping, the site now provides a more intuitive and engaging experience for potential clients.
  • Streamlined Content Management: The consolidated CMS makes it significantly easier for the team to update and maintain website content, reducing administrative overhead.
  • Enhanced Client Engagement: The redesigned site is optimized to maximize contacts with potential clients through improved user flows and strategic design elements.
  • Professional Growth: The success of this project led to receiving an offer and transitioning to a permanent part-time position, demonstrating the value delivered through this work.
