Project 1 - CPSC 581

I wanted to make something that utilized multiple media devices that are a part of a cellphone.

I knew that I was going to use the microphone, so I thought that if I could implement something with the camera as well I could make a voice controlled camera. That would be a pretty neat use case!

Promo Video

ActionShot is a photo application that takes a photo through a microphone volume-based trigger. Set up your phone during the big game and when your team scores and you cheer, ActionShot will capture the magic!

The idea came from wanting to capture those spontaneous moments of genuine excitement—the kind that are impossible to stage and easy to miss when you're busy fumbling for your camera.

Variations

After deciding on ActionShot, I explored different ways to implement the core concept of voice/audio-triggered photography.

Variation 1

Brainstorming possible UI layouts.

Variation 2

The "Cheese!" activated camera was a cool idea except for the stipulation in our project criteria that differentiating words cannot provide separate outputs from their inputs :(

Variation 3

Slight variation of the ActionShot idea but it takes a medley of photos that are stitched together to make a gif of your celebration!

Variation 4

Takes whatever you're saying as an input stream and creates a text file with all your words! While also recording a performance of you. The easiest audition recorder ever.

Variation 5

A little ambitious but similar to Snapchat filters. The pitch of your voice determines what filter is assigned to your face.

Variation 6

Sometimes all you can do is make a joke or embrace the awkwardness of an awkward silence. This captures the moment so you can cringe forever!

Variation 7

Ever taking a nap and you hear the oven go off to signal that your food might be done? Well this will send a photo of your food to your smart watch so you can check without even getting up!

Variation 8

Similar to the above idea, an application that can send photo/video footage to an internet-capable device of your choosing so you can observe your baby from afar.
